Timetable Upload

How to export Timetable CSV from Kamar
  1. In Kamar, open Reporting and select Timetable.
  2. Open the Teachers tab and choose Teacher Timetables (or Timetable Summary if your school uses that export).
  3. Set the timetable options for the term/week/cycle/day you want included.
  4. Open the Actions menu, choose Spreadsheet, then generate the export.
  5. In the Download Files popup, click the CSV filename to download it, then upload that file here.
