Student Upload

Upload a Student List CSV exported from Kamar. Expected columns (in any order): Student ID, Last Name, First Name, Gender, Level, Tutor, Timetable Class, Student email - School.

How to export StudentList.csv from Kamar
  1. In Kamar, open Reporting, then choose Student Lists.
  2. Select the list you want and include the required fields.
  3. Open Actions and choose Spreadsheet.
  4. Download the CSV and upload it here.
